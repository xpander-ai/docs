## Quick Setup Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Slack Templates" titleIcon="rocket">
    Visit [slack.xpander.ai](https://slack.xpander.ai/) and click **"Browse Templates"** to see ready-made agents.
    
    <Tip>Start with a template for the fastest setup - you can customize later!</Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Your Agent" titleIcon="robot">
    Pick from available templates:
    - **Support Agent** - Handle customer questions
    - **Docs Agent** - Answer questions from your documentation  
    - **General Assistant** - Multi-purpose helper
    
    Click **"Deploy to Slack"** on your chosen template.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Connect Your Slack" titleIcon="link">
    You'll be redirected to Slack to authorize the integration:
    1. Select your workspace
    2. Click **"Allow"** to grant permissions
    3. Choose which channels the agent can access (or start with DMs only)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Your Agent" titleIcon="message">
    Once connected, test your agent:
    1. Go to the connected Slack workspace
    2. Send a direct message to your new agent, or mention it in a channel
    3. Try: "Hello! Can you help me with something?"
    
    <Success>Your agent should respond within seconds!</Success>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize (Optional)" titleIcon="wrench">
    Want to tweak your agent? Go to your [xpander.ai console](https://app.xpander.ai/agents):
    - Modify the system prompt
    - Add custom tools and connectors
    - Configure auto-engagement rules
    
    Changes apply instantly to your Slack agent.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quick Tips for Success

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Make your agent more useful">
    - **Add context**: Include relevant company info in the system prompt
    - **Connect tools**: Add calendars, databases, or APIs for more capabilities
    - **Set boundaries**: Define what the agent should and shouldn't do
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Manage permissions">
    - Start with private channels or DMs to test
    - Add to public channels once you're confident
    - Use @mentions to control when the agent responds
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Scale your setup">
    - Create different agents for different teams
    - Use auto-engagement rules for specific topics
    - Connect multiple knowledge sources
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>

    - Verify permissions in the Slack app settings
    - Try sending a direct message first
  </Accordion>

    - Go to [xpander.ai console](https://app.xpander.ai/slack_agents)
